## What is TracksSource?

TracksSource is a **privacy-first, open-source attribution platform** designed for modern marketers and developers. It captures every customer touchpoint across your marketing channels and connects them to actual revenue.

## Why TracksSource?

### The Problem

Traditional analytics tools show you traffic, but not revenue. Ad platforms mark every conversion as their own. You're left wondering: **which marketing actually works?**

### The Solution

TracksSource connects the dots:

```
Ad Click → Website Visit → Form Submit → Purchase → Revenue
    ↓           ↓              ↓           ↓         ↓
  UTM       Anonymous ID    Lead Event   Purchase   Attribution
 Params      + Cookies       + Email     Event     Report
```

## Key Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Multi-Touch Attribution" icon="chart-line">

  <Accordion title="Identity Resolution" icon="user-group">
