# Quickstart Guide

Get TrackSource running on your website in under 5 minutes. By the end of this guide, you'll be tracking every visitor, lead, and conversion.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- A TrackSource account ([Sign up here](https://app.tracksource.io/signup))
- Access to your website's HTML or a tag manager
- Your form provider credentials (Tally, Typeform, etc.)

## Step 1: Get Your Tenant ID

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log into TrackSource">
    Go to [app.tracksource.io](https://app.tracksource.io) and sign in
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Your Tenant ID">
    Navigate to **Settings** → **General**. Your Tenant ID is displayed at the top.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy the ID">
    Click the copy button next to your Tenant ID. You'll need this for the next steps.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Install the Tracking Script

Add the TrackSource script to every page of your website. Place it in the `<head>` section:

```html
<script>
  (function(w,d,s,l,i){w[l]=w[l]||[];w[l].push({'ts.start':
  new Date().getTime(),event:'ts.js'});var f=d.getElementsByTagName(s)[0],
  j=d.createElement(s),dl=l!='tsLayer'?'&l='+l:'';j.async=true;j.src=
  'https://events.tracksource.io/sdk/ts.min.js?id='+i+dl;f.parentNode.insertBefore(j,f);
  })(window,document,'script','tsLayer','YOUR_TENANT_ID');
</script>
```

<Warning>
  Replace `YOUR_TENANT_ID` with your actual Tenant ID from Step 1.
</Warning>

### Platform-Specific Instructions

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="WordPress">
    1. Go to **Appearance** → **Theme Editor**
    2. Open `header.php`
    3. Paste the script before `</head>`
    4. Save changes

    Or use a plugin like **Insert Headers and Footers**.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Shopify">
    1. Go to **Online Store** → **Themes**
    2. Click **Actions** → **Edit code**
    3. Open `theme.liquid`
    4. Paste the script in the `<head>` section
    5. Save
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Webflow">
    1. Go to **Project Settings** → **Custom Code**
    2. Paste the script in **Head Code**
    3. Publish your site
  </Tab>

## Step 3: Configure Form Webhooks

Connect your form provider to TrackSource to capture leads automatically.

### Universal Webhook URL

Your webhook URL follows this pattern:

```
https://events.tracksource.io/webhooks/{provider}/YOUR_TENANT_ID
```

## Step 4: Add Hidden Fields (Recommended)

To ensure accurate attribution, add hidden fields to your forms that capture the TrackSource anonymous ID.

### For Tally Forms

Add a hidden field with the ID `ts_anonymous_id`. TrackSource will automatically populate it.

### For Custom Forms

```html
<form>
  <!-- Your form fields -->
  <input type="hidden" name="ts_anonymous_id" id="ts_anonymous_id" />

  <script>
    // Auto-populate the hidden field
    document.getElementById('ts_anonymous_id').value =
      window.tsLayer?.find(e => e.anonymous_id)?.anonymous_id || '';
  </script>
</form>
```

## Step 5: Verify Installation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Your Website">
    Visit your website in a new browser tab
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the Console">
    Open Developer Tools (F12) and look for `[TrackSource] Initialized`
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Live Events">
    Go to your TrackSource dashboard → **Analytics** → **Live Events**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit a Test Form">
    Fill out one of your forms and verify the lead appears in your dashboard
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Script not loading">
    - Check that your Tenant ID is correct
    - Ensure the script is in the `<head>` section
    - Verify no ad blockers are preventing the script from loading
    - Check browser console for errors
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Events not appearing in dashboard">
    - Wait 30 seconds after page load
    - Check that you're looking at the correct time range
    - Verify your Tenant ID matches between script and dashboard
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Form submissions not tracking">
    - Verify the webhook URL is correct
    - Check that your form provider's webhook is enabled
    - Look for errors in your form provider's webhook logs
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
